## Why this blog exists

This section is for short technical notes, project updates, measurement lessons, and practical RF design observations.

The files live in `src/blog` and are written in markdown. Each post can include a small frontmatter block with a title, date, and summary.

## Supported markdown

- Section headings
- Paragraphs with **bold**, *italic*, `inline code`, and [links](https://example.com)
- Bulleted lists
- Blockquotes
- Code fences

> Keep public posts free of proprietary, export-controlled, ITAR-controlled, classified, customer-identifying, or restricted details.

```text
Example measurement note:
Frequency: 5.7 GHz
Result: verify with calibrated bench setup before publishing
```
